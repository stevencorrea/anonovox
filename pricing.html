FAQ

Common questions

Can I stay on the free plan indefinitely?

Yes. The Trial plan is free forever with no time limit — as long as you stay within 10 users and 1 project. No credit card required.

How does annual billing work?

Annual billing is $50 per user per year, paid upfront. That's equivalent to $4.17/user/month — a 17% saving compared to the $5/month rate.

What counts as a "user" on the Pro plan?

A user is anyone with a login to your anonovox workspace — including leadership, admins, and employees who submit feedback under named accounts. Anonymous submitters are never counted.

How does EntraID enrollment work?

Enterprise accounts connect directly to your Microsoft EntraID (formerly Azure AD) tenant. Every person in your directory is automatically provisioned and kept in sync — no manual invite lists needed.

Is truly anonymous submission possible even for admins?

Yes. The feedback submission path is architecturally isolated from the identity layer. Even administrators cannot link a submission to an individual — only aggregate org-domain data is retained.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

You can upgrade at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Annual plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled before renewal.
