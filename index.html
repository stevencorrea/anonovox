Process

Three steps from honest thought to organizational clarity

01
Submit

Anyone in the organization shares what they actually think — no account, no identity, no risk. Just an honest voice entering the system.

02
Batch & deliver

We anonymize, curate, and group submissions into digestible batches delivered directly to leadership on a regular cadence. Nothing is lost. Nothing is filtered out.

03
Respond publicly

Leadership responds to individual pieces of feedback through the platform. Every response is published for the entire organization — creating real, visible accountability.

Why anonovox

Built on trust. Designed for honesty.

True anonymity

All identifying metadata is stripped before delivery — no IP addresses, no timestamps, no behavioral patterns. Employees speak without fear of retaliation or exposure.

Guaranteed delivery

Leadership can't ignore what they can't avoid. Every submission is tracked from receipt to confirmed delivery. No feedback quietly disappears.

Public accountability
